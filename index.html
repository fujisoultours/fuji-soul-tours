Before You Go

Important Notes

💴
Bring cash (JPY)

Some spots are cash-only — lockers, kimono rental, shrine offerings, and certain cafés. Most restaurants and shops also accept cards.

🚅
Take the Kodama (こだま)

Only Kodama trains stop at Shin-Fuji Station — not Nozomi or Hikari. Covered by JR Pass and usually less crowded.

🗻
Best seat for Mt. Fuji views on the Shinkansen

Sit in seat E from Tokyo (right-side window), or seat A from Kyoto/Osaka (left-side window) for the best Mt. Fuji views from the bullet train.
