Questions

Frequently asked questions

How does the group tour work?

We run a small group tour every Wednesday, October to December 2026. Pick the Wednesday you want and book — USD 98 per person (all ages), charged at booking with instant confirmation (up to 15 guests). The tour departs once 10 guests are booked for that date. If fewer than 10 guests are booked 72 hours before departure, you choose a free transfer to another Wednesday or a full refund.

Can I cancel or get a refund?

Bookings are non-refundable. The one exception: if fewer than 10 guests are booked 72 hours before departure, the tour doesn't run — and you choose a free transfer to another Wednesday or a full refund of everything you paid.

How is this different from the private tour?

The private tour (max 5 guests) is fully customizable — you choose your time, stops and pace, in about 4 hours. The group tour is a shared 6-hour experience on a Wednesday model course, with the same local-born guide, by microbus, at a lower per-person price.

What's included in the price?

The price covers your English-speaking local-born guide and private microbus transport for the day. Lunch, tea, entrance fees and other on-site costs are not included — you pay for those as you go.

What if my Wednesday doesn't fill up?

If fewer than 10 guests are booked 72 hours before departure, we'll contact you and you choose: a free transfer to another Wednesday, or a full refund of everything you paid. No risk to you.

How much is it?

USD 98 per person — one flat price for all ages. Lunch, tea, entrance fees and other on-site costs are not included; you pay for those as you go on the day.

Want fully customized dates and route?

Our 4-hour Private Tour runs any day of the week — your choice of stops, your pace, your van. Same Shin-Fuji meet, same Fuji-area guides.
