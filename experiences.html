Try Japan's best green tea

Hand-picked, organic, non-fertilised green tea — a Prime Minister's Award winner. Drink it, then season and eat the steeped leaves, with hand-made traditional sweets on the side. Just tea, savoured at the café.

Wagashi sweets workshop

Shape seasonal Japanese sweets from white bean paste — nerikiri, a craft born in Edo-period Kyoto two to three centuries ago and refined alongside the tea ceremony.

¥3,000 / person60 min
