Frequently Asked Questions

We run a small group tour every Wednesday from October 2026. You join the waitlist for the Wednesday you want — it's free. Once 10 adults are signed up for that date, we confirm the tour (up to 15 guests) and send you secure checkout. You only pay once your Wednesday is confirmed.

The private tour (max 5 guests) is fully customizable — you choose your time, stops and pace, in about 4 hours. The group tour is a shared 6-hour experience on a set Wednesday route, with the same local-born guide, by microbus, at a lower per-person price.

The price covers your English-speaking local-born guide and private microbus transport for the day. Lunch, tea, entrance fees and other on-site costs are not included — you pay for those as you go.

If a Wednesday doesn't reach 10 adults, you won't be charged. We'll offer you priority on the next Wednesday or a special rate on a private tour. No risk to you.

Adults are $98 per person and children (6–12) are $49. As a launch offer, every Wednesday in October 2026 is 30% off — Adult $68, Child $34. Standard pricing applies from November.
