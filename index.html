Itinerary

Tour Stops

7 handpicked locations — your guide will select the best combination based on your preferences and conditions.

01

Shiraito Falls

Mt. Fuji's snowmelt emerges as thousands of fine "white threads" forming a broad curtain of water. ~20 min · Free

02

Fujisan Hongu Sengen Taisha Shrine

The principal shrine for Mt. Fuji worship. Walk beneath vermilion gates and cedars to Wakutama-ike spring. ~20 min · Free

03

Omiya Yokocho — Fujinomiya Yakisoba

Award-winning local soul food. Chewy steamed noodles on a sizzling griddle with pork cracklings. ~20 min

04

Fuji Takasago Sake Brewery

The same snowmelt from Mt. Fuji becomes sake. Taste several varieties and learn about water softness & rice polishing. ~20 min · Free

05

Kobayashien — Tea Experience

Authentic Shizuoka green tea nurtured by Mt. Fuji's spring water. Traditional Japanese house overlooking a garden. ~20 min

06

Fujiyama Hunter's Beer Tap Room

Local small-batch craft beers brewed with Mt. Fuji spring water and organic local grains. 6–7 rotating taps. ~20 min

07

Lake Tanuki

Mt. Fuji reflected on still waters — the stunning "Double Fuji" mirror image. Weather permitting. ~20 min · Free
