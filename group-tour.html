Frequently Asked Questions

We gather interested travelers into groups of 10–20. Once enough people sign up, we confirm a tour date and send you booking details. Joining the waitlist is completely free — you only pay once the tour is confirmed.

The private tour (max 5 guests) is fully customizable — you choose your time, stops, and pace. The group tour is a shared experience with a set itinerary, longer duration (6 hours vs 4), and a more affordable per-person price. Same guide, same quality, different vibe.

If a group doesn't reach 10 people, you won't be charged. We'll offer you priority placement on the next group or a special offer on a private tour. No risk to you.

Pricing will be shared with waitlist members first — before it goes public. Waitlist members also get early-bird pricing. Join now to lock in the best rate.
